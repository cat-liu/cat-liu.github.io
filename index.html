My name is Cathy and I’m savvy in a wide variety of disciplines. Throughout my career, my managers and peers have thought of me as a “swiss-army knife”.

My latest professional journey was co-founding Buji, a consumer marketplace startup where we learned the stories of more than 250 local merchants to help them connect with their audience and grow their businesses through livestream video.

Before Buji, my career took some interesting turns based on my interests. I enjoy learning about tech cultures in other countries, so I spent a summer in Shanghai in product at Didi diving deep into China's car rental scene. We launched a car rental product, in collaboration with Avis, to 175 countries internationally.

My curiosity in supply-chain operations grew during my time at UC San Diego, where I studied Management Science with a secondary in Cognitive Science. This path led me to business operations at Intel where I was trusted to oversee the management of 18 client and data center product lines.

I find great fulfillment in using my skills to support those in need and had the unique opportunity to work with Sam Altman and ship product design for his open source project founded during the 2020 pandemic, helpwithcovid.com.

While I am not working, I enjoy traveling around the world, working on my wellness in pilates classes, learning about film photography, discovering lesser known musicians, and indulging in provocative Netflix documentaries.

I love consumer marketplaces and am curious about adjacent sectors that could transform the space, such as fintech, AI, social media, and logistics. I am currently located in SF Bay Area. I am open to chatting about new ideas, please reach out!
